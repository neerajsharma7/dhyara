Home Remedies For Perfect Hair Straightening


Many times when we want a perfect look for a party all we can think of is shiny straight hair and we are all good to go! The biggest advantage of having straight hair is you get zero-maintenance hair styles easily. All you need to do is to have a good hair wash and you are ready for any occasion.

Having straight hair is a good deal. Be it any party or formal occasion, you can perform almost any hairstyle on straight hair and you are ready to be the center of attraction. This is what curly hair girls don’t really get. What when you don’t have perfectly straight hair and you need them? That means you need to get your hair straight!



When it comes to getting your hair straight, the things that come to our minds are harmful heat based products and techniques. Permanent hair straightening from salon gives you the expected results for limited time. But, after sometime, you will end up with even more bad conditioned and damaged hairs. But what if you can get healthy straight hair naturally at home without any heat and mess? Well, there are some home remedies, which can straighten your hair, permanently and naturally.

Below I have summarized 10 home remedies for natural hair straightening.

1. Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera as we all have heard does wonder to the skin. But not everyone knows that its magical properties are not just limited to the skin!

Aloe Vera keeps hair moisturized and due to this, it can straighten hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Take one cup of Aloe Vera gel and mix it with warm olive oil.

2. Massage this mixture into your scalp and hair.

3. Leave it for about one to two hours.

4. Rinse it off using a mild shampoo.


2. Fuller’s earth (Multani Mitti)

Fuller’s earth is popularly known as Multani Mitti in India. Along with its marvelous effects on skin, it can help straighten hair that too naturally. It is a mild cleansing agent and helps get rid of frizzy hair too. It doesn’t damage hair.

Usage :

1. Take one cup of fuller’s earth, mix it with one egg and two tablespoons of rice flour.

2. Add water to give a thin texture.

3. Apply this thin paste on your hair and comb your hair (use a wide-toothed comb).

4. Leave for one hour and rinse it with water.

5. Spray milk on your hair and leave for 15 minutes.

6. Now you can shampoo and condition your hair.


3. Coconut Milk

Coconut milk is well known for its moisturizing ability and is believed to straighten hair naturally. It provides hair with beautiful shine. It has antifungal, antibacterial and antiviral benefits and this keeps your scalp free from any sort of infection.

Usage :

1. Mix one cup of fresh coconut milk with juice of one lemon in a glass jar.

2. Keep the jar in the refrigerator for a few hours, until you observe a creamy layer on the top.

3. Massage your scalp and hair with the cream, and leave it for 20 minutes.

4. Cover your head with a shower cap and wrap a towel soaked in warm water around it.

5. Leave it on for 30 minutes.

6. Now you can shampoo and condition your hair.


4. Eggs and olive oil

Eggs and olive oil help make hair stronger, shinier and keep hair well moisturized. Eggs and Olive oil when combined help to straighten the hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Beat two eggs and add four tablespoons of olive oil to it and mix well.

2. Apply the mixture on your scalp and hair and comb your hair (use a wide-toothed comb).

3. Leave it for 30-45 minutes.

4. Rinse it and wash your hair with mild shampoo.


5. Beer

Beer is rich in vitamins and proteins. It acts as a cleansing agent and provides shine to the hair. It acts as a remedy to straighten your hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Boil a cup of beer over medium heat, till it reduces to half.

2. Cool it.

3. Mix it with a cup of shampoo and shampoo your hair with this mixture.

4. The boiled beer can be used as a conditioner too.


6. Eggs

Eggs alone can be used to straighten hair.

Usage :

1. Take eggs and whip them.

2. When they turn smooth, apply on hair and scalp.

3. Leave for 30 minutes and wash your hair with a mild shampoo.


7. Milk and honey

Milk and honey, when used together, can work to straighten hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Take a cup of milk; add 2 tablespoons of honey to it.

2. You can add some fresh strawberries to this mix.

3. Apply this on your hair.

4. Leave it for minimum 2 hours.

5. Rinse using a mild shampoo.


8. Vinegar

Vinegar does wonders to hair just as it does wonders to the food. Vinegar contributes to the straightening of hair.

Usage :

1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

2. After washing your hair, add a few drops of Vinegar in a mug filled with water.

3. Rinse the hair with this water.

4. After rinsing the hair with Vinegar- water mix, rinse it with clean water.


9. Castor Oil

Castor Oil is obtained from castor beans and offers amazing benefits to skin, health and even hair. Castor oil can be used as a home remedy to straighten hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Take castor oil and warm it.

2. While the oil is warm, apply it on the scalp and massage it for few minutes.

3. Wrap your hair in a towel soaked in warm water for some time.

4. Rinse the hair after 30-40 minutes.


10. Celery

Celery can be used to straighten hair naturally.

Usage :

1. Take a bunch of celery and extract its juice.

2. Store it in a bottle and leave it overnight.

3. Apply this juice from roots to tip.

4. Comb your hair and cover them with a shower cap.

5. Leave this as it is for 30 minutes.

6. Wash your hair.
