Get Rid of Dark Circles with these Easy to use Home Remedies


Dark circles, also known as dark rings or shadows are truly one of the most challenging issues faced by individuals nowadays. They are easy to come, but very rigid to get rid of. They do not only make you look dull, but degrade your overall appearance as well. As you grow older, your skin becomes thinner and loses collagen, which is also one of the reasons we get dark circles under eyes.

Causes of Dark Circles

There are lots of factors responsible for causing Dark circles. Some of them include:

  Lack of nutrition in the diet
  Dehydration and malnutrition
  Anemia and Aging
  Lack of proper sleep
  Mental and physical stress
  Injuries around the eyes
  Scratching or rubbing eyes too often
  Chronic skin condition
  Fatigue and long working hours

Symptoms of Dark Circles

Symptoms of Dark Circles can vary from person to person. Some face it due to lack of nutrition in their diet, whereas some due to their inability to sleep. But sometimes it also occurs with many other signs and symptoms like:

  Swelling and under eye puffiness
  Itchy eyes and nasal congestion
  Fatigue, irritation and anxiety due to lack of proper sleep
  Sinus infection and Severe Headache
  Chronic skin conditions like Eczema and atopic dermatitis
  Shortness of breathing and wheezing

Remedies to treat Dark Circles Naturally

The best and most effective way to treat dark circles naturally is to use Home remedies that are not only natural, but are 100% safe for your skin as well as eyes. The ingredient used in these remedies is mostly available at home, which makes them affordable too and save you from buying expensive skin products.

So if you too have a dark circle and looking for ways to get fast and effective results, then here are some quick home remedies that you should definitely try to get rid of Dark circles naturally:

1. Cucumber

Cucumber is full of useful properties. It also acts as a skin lightening agent and contains mild astringent properties that make your skin soft, glowing and smooth. It is also highly effective to treat dark circles and spots.
Method to use:

Take a fresh cucumber and cut it into slices. Now place the slices in the refrigerator and chill them for 30 minutes. Put these slices on the affected area and leave it for15 minutes. Let your skin absorb the nutrients and then wash it off. Repeat the process regularly for two weeks or more.

2. Tomato

Tomatoes are one of the best food that contains rich amounts of probiotics and are great for maintaining good skin health. They are also very beneficial to lighten skin and works best to reduce dark circles under eyes.
Method to use:

Take 1 teaspoon of tomato juice and mix it with half teaspoon of lemon juice. Apply this mixture under your eyes and leave it to dry for 10 minutes, then wash it off with water. Follow this remedy twice in a week for a month or more.

3. Lemon Juice

Rich in antioxidants and Vitamin C, Lemon juice can be used to treat various skin conditions. The skin lightening properties of Lemon juice help in reducing dark circles to a great extent.
Method to use:

Take a fresh lemon juice and apply the juice all around your eyes. Leave it for around 10 minutes and wash it off. Follow this remedy twice a week for fast results. In case of burning sensation and irritation, discontinue using.

4. Cold Compress

This is also one of the most preferred and effective ways to treat dark circles. Another advantage of using cold compress is that it constricts the blood vessels and reduce the dark circles and puffiness around the skin.
Method to use:

All you need to do is to soak a clean cloth or hand towel in cold water or cold milk. Put in on closed eyes for 2-3 minutes. You can also take help of ice cubes. Wrap some ice cubes in a soft napkin or hand towel and then place it on your eyes for a few minutes. You can try any of the above mentioned techniques.

5. Rose Water

From toning to skin cleansing, Rose water is truly a savior when it comes to skin. You can also use it to reduce dark circles under eyes. It keeps the pores clean and rejuvenate your skin from both inside and outside.
Method to use:

Soak cotton eye pads or cotton balls in Rose water for 2 minutes. Take it out and place it on your closed eyelids. Leave it on your eyes for 15 minutes. Follow this remedy twice a week for a month and see the results.

6. Almond Oil

This oil is great for all skin types. Rich in essential nutrients and its beneficial properties makes this oil, one of the best oil to treat dark circles. It also acts as an emollient that prevents dry, flaky skin and prevents skin spots.
Method to use:

Take 1 tsp Almond oil and gently massage it under your eyes on dark circles. You can follow this remedy before going to bed every night until your dark circles disappear.

So try out these remedies to stay away from puffy eyes and dark circles and make sure you sleep for at least 7-8 hours regularly to prevent dark circles under eyes. It is one of the most important factors to get flawless skin. Also remember to include foods that are rich in Vitamin A, Vitamin K and Vitamin E for maintaining healthy skin. So eat well, sleep well and look great always.
